Jing Ren Senior Researcher, ETH Zurich

I am currently a senior researcher in the Interactive Geometry Lab, ETH Zurich, advised by Prof. Olga Sorkine-Hornung. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Visual Computing Center, KAUST, advised by Prof. Peter Wonka and Prof. Maks Ovsjanikov. Before that, I obtained the M.Sc. degree from the University of Oxford, UK, and the B.Sc. degree from Zhejiang University, China. My research is mainly related to shape analysis, geometry processing and digital fabrication. I am also interested in computer graphics and computer vision in general.

Note: I will be traveling from mid-February to mid-May 2026. During this period, due to limited internet access and availability, I will be unable to accept new review invitations and may be slow in responding to emails. Starting from mid-May 2026, I will be joining the Université de Montréal as an Assistant Professor. If you are interested in pursuing a Master or PhD in geometry processing and/or digital fabrication, please feel free to contact me via email with your academic CV (please forgive my deplayed reply during this period).
